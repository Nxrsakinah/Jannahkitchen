  • CHOCOLATES

    At Jannah's Kitchen, we take pride in creating exceptional baked goods that ignite your taste buds and bring pure joy. We believe that using the finest ingredients is key to achieving the perfect balance of flavor, texture, and satisfaction. That's why we turn to Valrhona, a world-renowned chocolatier, to provide us with the exquisite chocolates that elevate our creations to new heights.

    For our milk chocolate lovers, we turn to Valrhona's Pure Origin Bar Andoa Milk with 35% cacao. This melt-in-the-mouth chocolate boasts an intensely milky flavor and cocoa richness that will leave you craving for more. With notes of farm milk and a hint of acidulous undertones, this chocolate adds a delightful complexity to our recipes.

    Indulge in the depth and richness of Valrhona's Pure Origin Bar Dark Araguani 100% for our dark chocolate creations. Its touch of vanilla, woody notes, and hint of bitterness take you on an adventure through the breathtaking landscapes of Venezuela. This full-bodied chocolate elevates our baked goods to new heights, leaving a lingering cocoa sensation on your palate.

    For our white chocolate treats, we rely on Valrhona's Bar Gourmet Ivoire. Smooth and delicately sweet, Ivoire white chocolate offers a fresh milky taste complemented by a delicate hint of vanilla. It brings a touch of elegance and creaminess to our recipes, enchanting your senses with its luscious flavor.

  • FRUITS

    We are proud to showcase the best of Malaysia's tropical bounty. Our commitment to quality starts with sourcing the finest fruits right here in our beloved country. For our pineapple lovers, we handpick the juiciest and most flavorful pineapples from local farms across Malaysia. Bursting with sweet and tangy goodness, these locally sourced pineapples lend a tropical twist to our creations, delivering a taste that truly embodies the essence of Malaysia. Similarly, our strawberries are carefully selected from local growers who nurture these berries with utmost care. The result is a delightful burst of sweetness in every bite, showcasing the natural beauty of Malaysian strawberries."

    While we celebrate the best of Malaysia, we also believe in embracing the global diversity of flavors. To bring you an even wider range of taste sensations, we source select fruits from around the world. From the United States, we import an array of fruits, including cherries, blueberries, and raspberries, renowned for their exceptional quality and distinctive flavors. These internationally sourced fruits add a delightful dimension to our offerings, complementing our locally sourced treasures. By curating the finest imported fruits, we aim to provide you with a taste adventure that transcends borders, bringing you a delightful fusion of flavors from near and far."

  • DAIRY-PRODUCTS

    We take pride in using dairy products sourced exclusively from local farms. We believe in supporting our local community and celebrating the rich agricultural heritage of our region. That's why every delightful bite of our baked goods is infused with the freshness and quality that comes from the finest local dairy ingredients.

    Our commitment to using local dairy extends to the creamy butter and luscious cream that we incorporate into our recipes. By partnering with nearby farms, we ensure that our dairy ingredients are produced with care and adhere to the highest standards of quality. This not only allows us to create delicious treats but also promotes sustainability and fosters a closer connection between our customers and the local farming community.

  • PROTEIN

    We prioritize sustainability and quality in every aspect of our culinary creations. That's why we source our protein from local farm butchers, ensuring that our ingredients are not only fresh and flavorful but also meet the highest standards of halal certification.

    We take pride in supporting local farmers who share our commitment to ethical and responsible practices. Our partnership with trusted local farm butchers allows us to deliver a diverse range of protein options that are not only delicious but also adhere to the strict guidelines of halal certification.

  • SPICES

    We understand that spices are the soul of any culinary masterpiece. That's why we take great care in sourcing premium and locally produced spices to infuse our creations with captivating flavors.

    We believe in supporting local spice producers who share our passion for quality and authenticity.   • RICE

    Royal Umbrella is synonymous with exceptional rice products, and their glutinous rice is no exception. With their unwavering commitment to quality and meticulous cultivation methods, Royal Umbrella produces a wide range of rice varieties, including a superior glutinous rice ideal for creating Pulut Kuning.

    Our Pulut Kuning, a traditional Malaysian delicacy, boasts the perfect balance of sticky texture and fragrant aroma, thanks to the exceptional quality of Royal Umbrella glutinous rice. Each grain is carefully selected to ensure consistent cookability and superb taste, elevating the dining experience to new heights.
