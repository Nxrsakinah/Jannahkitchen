Our history

Our Sweet Journey Begins

Founded in 2020, Jannah's Kitchen was brought to life by our visionary founder, Jannah. Fueled by her love for baking and inspired by the joy it brings, Jannah embarked on a mission to create exceptional baked goods that would leave a lasting impression on every palate.

Jannah's Kitchen has expanded its reach, captivating the hearts and taste buds of dessert enthusiasts far and wide.

What started as a small baking endeavor in Selangor,quickly gained popularity for its delectable creations and unwavering commitment to quality.

Savor the Evolution: From Cookies to Culinary Delights!

Today, Jannah's Kitchen has blossomed into a haven of culinary delights. While cookies remain close to our hearts, our menu has expanded to include a plethora of mouthwatering creations. From delicate tarts to savory sticky rice, we offer a diverse range of culinary delights that will satisfy even the most discerning palates.

Continuing the Sweet Success, Stronger Than Ever in 2023!

Now, 2023, we stand stronger than ever, fueled by the love and support of our cherished customers. Through their trust and appreciation, we continue to refine our craft, explore new flavors, and push the boundaries of culinary artistry.

As we move forward, our commitment to using the finest ingredients, crafting with precision, and providing exceptional service remains unshakable. With each passing day, we aim to elevate the culinary experience, making every moment spent with Jannah's Kitchen a sweet and memorable one.
